Our Mission

Vault + Vine endeavors to engage our local community in meaningful ways. Through volunteerism, financial support, involvement in community groups, and providing a safe space for all to gather, we aim to provide the best of what the world has to offer to you.

Peicha

Peicha Chang is the founder of Vault + Vine. In 2009, she founded Falls Flowers, a floral design shop in East Falls, before transitioning the business to Vault + Vine in 2017. Before Falls Flowers, Peicha was a designer and manager at Bow Street Flowers in Boston, Massachusetts. At Bow Street, Peicha honed her design sensibilities and discovered her love of garden style design.

Peicha is a graduate of Smith College in Northampton, Massachusetts. She lives with her family in East Falls, where she’s able to walk to work and share her lifelong love of plants and flowers with her community.

Our Story

Vault + Vine is the re-envisioning of Peicha Chang’s Falls Flowers. After spending the last five years at their little storefront around the corner on Conrad Street, Peicha and her team have outgrown the space and have committed to their new location here on Midvale to offer an immersive retail experience while providing the community with a cafe, gathering place, and small scale event venue.

Our customers are friends and neighbors who, over the years, have become family. Local creators, whose hands are part of every process of making their goods, produce many of the objects we carry. The seasonal flowers we use in our arrangements are grown in and around Philadelphia at small farms, and are harvested and delivered to us by the same farmers who planted the seeds. Any of our products or flowers not locally produced or grown are always sourced responsibly and ethically.

Our Designers

Kat

Kat’s love of flowers started while she was a grower, managing a 3-acre cut flower farm in Doylestown, PA. Through farming, she formed relationships with floral designers in Philadelphia and discovered a love for floral design. In 2016 she started From Blossoms, her own floral design/flower csa business, based out of Germantown.

Meg

Meg is a born-and-raised East Fallser! She can be seen walking her pup Noah around the neighborhood, enjoying margaritas at Taqueria Feliz, or creating fabulous arrangements for events here at Vault + Vine. Around the shop she likes to chat about movies and podcasts. Her favorite flowers are ranunculus for their delicacy and straw flowers for their iridescence.

Sarah

Sarah combines her education in illustration and floriculture to create seasonally-focused narrative designs. She spends much of her time outside of the shop either in the garden or making puppets.

Why We're Different

We're a B-Corperation

Falls Flowers was the first floral-retail and event design studio to ‘live” sustainably as one of 1,600+ businesses worldwide that is a certified B-Corp, and Vault + Vine aims to continue that legacy.

A B-Corp is a for-profit company certified by the nonprofit B Lab to meet rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. Our motto is “People using business as a force for good,’ with a focus on a triple bottom line: people, planet, AND profit.

We strive to do what a good business should do: support our community, buy and source locally, pay our employees well, and follow high standards of sustainability.
